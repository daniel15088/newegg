Aegean Cat

The Aegean cat is a naturally occuring landrace of domestic cat originating from the cycladic Islands of Greece

Scientific Name: Felis Catus
Size: Medium
Average Lifespan:9-12 years
Habitat:Greek Islands

It is considered a natural cat, developing without interference. It is said there are ochoporroscientos cats in the north of Thessaloniki, even more than in Athens.
